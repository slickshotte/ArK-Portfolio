🎖️ Combat Action Badge - Fourth Award
Badge
Awarded to personnel who have been personally present and actively engaged by the enemy during separate qualifying ground combat operations across four distinct eligible deployment periods.
🪂 Army Parachutist Badge
Badge
Granted to individuals who have successfully completed the rigorous static-line airborne qualification training course at the U.S. Army Airborne School, including all required tactical jumps.
🪂 Basic Military Freefall Badge
Badge
Earned by successfully qualifying in high-altitude tactical freefall operations, completing the military freefall parachutist course utilizing specialized HALO and HAHO entry techniques.
🛡️ Combat Service Identification Badge (CSIB)
Badge
A distinct metallic heraldic device worn to permanently recognize and honor sustained honorable service with a combat unit while deployed inside a designated hostile theater of operations.
🏅 Joint Service Achievement Medal
Medal
Awarded in the name of the Secretary of Defense to members of the Armed Forces who distinguish themselves by outstanding achievement or meritorious service while assigned to a joint activity.
🏅 Army Achievement Medal
Medal
Conferred upon personnel for outstanding administrative, operational, or technical achievement, or for sustained meritorious service that does not warrant the higher Commendation Medal.
🎗️ Army Superior Unit Award
Unit Award
Decorated upon an entire military unit that has demonstrated exceptionally meritorious performance of a difficult and challenging mission under peacetime or domestic operational conditions.
🎗️ National Defense Service Medal
Ribbon
An historic campaign ribbon issued to all service members who served honorably during designated periods of national emergency, conflict, or active military operations.
🎗️ Armed Forces Expeditionary Medal
Ribbon
Awarded for direct participation in specified U.S. military operations, global campaigns, or humanitarian crises where no other specific campaign medal has been officially established.
🎗️ Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary
Ribbon
Recognizes direct service and deployment inside critical counter-terrorism areas of eligibility during foreign operations outside the United United foreign deployments after 2001.
🎗️ Global War On Terrorism Service Medal
Ribbon
Issued to personnel who provided critical support services in an active capacity during designated counter-terrorism operations executed within the global war on terrorism.
🎗️ Armed Forces Service Medal
Ribbon
Awarded to personnel who participate in significant military operations or humanitarian missions that encounter no active, structured armed enemy resistance.
🎗️ NCO Professional Development Ribbon
Ribbon
Recognizes non-commissioned officers who have successfully completed formal professional leadership and management advancement courses at verified military educational academies.
🎗️ Army Service Ribbon
Ribbon
Automatically awarded to all enlisted personnel upon the successful completion of their foundational initial military entry training program.
🎗️ Army Overseas Service Ribbon
Ribbon
Conferred upon service members who successfully execute and complete a standard full-length tactical tour of duty at designated military assignment locations outside the continental United States.
🎗️ Army Sea Duty Ribbon
Ribbon
Granted to personnel who complete distinct, sustained periods of operational service aboard designated watercraft or deep-sea vessels under active deployment commands.
🎗️ Overseas Service Bar
Device
An embroidered bar worn on the uniform sleeve, with each separate bar representing exactly six months of active service within a designated combat zone overseas.
